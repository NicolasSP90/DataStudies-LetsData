Objective
- From the album cardboards of the FIFA World Cup 2022, extract the player statistics and image. The statistics do not show the player's Club or its value.

The project follows this steps:
1- Capture frames from the webcam (OpenCV)
2- When pressing the "c" key, the OCR will capture the frames
3- When pressing the "s" word, search for the player statistics
4- Writte the data in the frame ()
5- Presents the frame with its data
6- When pressing the "e" word, erase all data

Data: https://www.kaggle.com/code/raphaelmarconato/fifa-23-players-and-teams-eda/data
